Homestay

Homestay + Intensive courses (B1, B2, C1)

Our Russian courses are perfect for people, learning the language for work, for study or simply for personal interest. As they are designed for everyone who wants to improve fluency and accuracy in Russian.

One of the best ways to understand the local culture is to stay with one of our homestay families. Staying in a homestay gives you a unique view of Russian speaking family life, the way that we communicate, the topics we talk about and Kazakh culture!

All our host families like to talk, share, and exchange. Our families are aware that you come to Kazakhstan to improve your Russian, and therefore that it is important for you to communicate in Russian, whatever your level may be.

All our host families have been selected for accommodation quality and commitment. They are always happy to share their culture and way of life with our participants. Families always host our participants at clean and comfortable accommodation. All accommodations are located in safe neighborhoods.

Host families provide:

  • An accommodation in a private room.
  • Only one student per family.
  • Wi-Fi internet access in all our host families.
  • Taxi transfers included from/to the airport.
  • An apartment key provided upon arrival
  • Desk, chair, desk lamp, dresser
  • Breakfast and evening dinner on weekdays. 3 meals per day on weekend

We select only families motivated to exchange with foreigners, ready to spend time with you and share their daily lives. Our families are not only a place where you spend the night, but above all a place where you communicate, meet Russian speakers and practice the language. We know each family personally and we visit them regularly. We have an official contract with each family. All host families own apartments and do not rent out a room to anyone at the same time.

When you fill in your application, please mention your preferences so that we can provide you with the best option for your stay. The sooner you register; the sooner we will start looking for the best homestay option available for you. We want your homestay experience to be as best as possible.
