Charity

Terra Education Language School has been conducting Annual Charity Fairs to raise money for kids with rare diseases since 2012. Terra Team sells pastries, sweets, handmade items, books, accessories, and organizes workshops with clay toys for children. All the money raised is donated to families whose children require expensive surgeries and treatment. Terra Education has been able to raise around 32,000 USD for children with rare diseases.

The school has been involved in supporting various community projects through donations and sponsorships. The Hora project, which aims to help youth development and fight against addictions, the Rotary club Astana in providing strollers for children with cerebral palsy. Terra Education supported the Orange Gala dinner organized by the Embassy of the Kingdom of the Netherlands and ASA (The Ambassadors Spouses Association Kazakhstan) for the Annual Charity Bazaar. In total, Terra Education donated around 8,000 USD for the abovementioned social projects.

Terra Education organized the Charity Auction that raised 1,700 USD for pet shelters. Additionally, the company participated in a handmade workshop to donate money for the reconstruction of destroyed public schools in Ukraine. Terra Education also regularly provides assistance to low-income families with groceries, clothes, and toys.

Receptions

The atmosphere at Terra Education is described as warm, welcoming, and full of positivity. This is exemplified by a conversation between a little student and a teacher, where the student noticed that everyone was smiling and asked why. The teacher responded by saying that it was because everyone was happy in our school. The staff at Terra Education is known for their caring, loving, and supportive nature, not just towards each other, but also towards their students, partners, and friends. This positive energy is reflected in their enjoyment of work and their willingness to spend their free time together, exploring new places, hobbies, and activities. Students call this energy "Magic of Terra Education".
